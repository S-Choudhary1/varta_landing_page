WhatsApp Cloud API approval guide

A short checklist to help teams prepare for approvals and keep messaging quality high.

1. Validate business details

Confirm legal business name, website, and customer support contact are consistent across all systems.

2. Capture consent the right way

Use clear opt-in language, store timestamps, and provide a simple opt-out path in every conversation.

3. Submit policy-safe templates

Templates should align with approved categories and avoid restricted or promotional content where prohibited.

4. Monitor quality signals

Track complaint rates, opt-outs, and response times to protect account standing.

5. Document the process

Keep audit trails for approvals, templates, and campaign activity for fast compliance reviews.
