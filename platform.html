Product details

A complete operating system for WhatsApp CRM teams.

Flowstack CRM combines inbox management, automation, templates, and compliance controls into one workspace to support WhatsApp Cloud API approvals and daily operations.

Core modules

  • Omnichannel inbox (WhatsApp-first)
  • Automation builder
  • Campaigns & broadcasts
  • Templates & approval tracking
  • Analytics & QA

Modules built for enterprise-ready WhatsApp operations

Every module is aligned to reduce approval risk, improve customer experience, and ensure teams stay compliant.

Inbox & routing

Assign conversations by skill, schedule, or priority. Use SLAs, collision prevention, and supervisor oversight.

Automation builder

Design flows for onboarding, reminders, and renewals with policy-safe templates and opt-out triggers.

Campaign studio

Create bulk sends with rate controls, quiet hours, and engagement scoring to protect quality.

Template governance

Track status, approvals, and edits. Maintain visibility for every template across teams and regions.

Customer 360
