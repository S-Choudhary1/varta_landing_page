Common questions

Quick answers for teams evaluating a WhatsApp CRM.

Does Flowstack replace the WhatsApp Business Platform?

No. Flowstack is a CRM layer that helps manage automation and compliance on top of the WhatsApp Cloud API.

Can the team manage template approvals?

Yes. The template studio tracks submission status, versions, and performance for each template.

How are opt-outs handled?

Customers can opt out at any time, and Flowstack enforces opt-out rules across campaigns and journeys.

Is bulk messaging supported?

Yes, but all bulk sends are gated behind approved templates and safety throttles.
